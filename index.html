Pillars of Bangladesh 2.0

Innovation

Bangladesh is embracing innovation in various sectors, including technology, agriculture, and healthcare, to drive sustainable development and economic growth.

sustainability

Bangladesh is committed to sustainable development practices, integrating environmental considerations into its national development plans and promoting green technologies and renewable energy sources.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is investing heavily in infrastructure development, including transportation networks, digital connectivity, and urban planning, to support economic growth and improve quality of life.

Empowerment

Bangladesh is working towards empowering all citizens, especially women and marginalized communities, by providing equal opportunities in education, employment, and political participation.

Vision of Future

The vision for Bangladesh 2.0 is to transform the country into a digitally empowered, sustainable, and inclusive society by 2030. promoting innovation, sustainability, and infrastructure development. With exclusive focus on innovation and sustainability, Bangladesh aims to become a leading nation in South Asia.

Digital Transformation

Bangladesh aims to become a digital hub in South Asia, leveraging technology to enhance governance, education, healthcare, and economic opportunities for all citizens.

Digital Bangladesh Initiative

    Date: January 26, 2009 Time: 9:00 AM

    The Digital Bangladesh initiative aims to transform Bangladesh into a knowledge-based society by
    leveraging information and communication technologies (ICT). The government has launched various

    for all citizens.
